Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a request?

We aim to respond to all requests within 30 calendar days. Complex requests or those requiring additional verification may take up to 60 days, but we'll keep you informed throughout the process.

Is there a fee for data requests?

No, data detection requests are free of charge. However, we may charge a reasonable fee if your request is manifestly unfounded, excessive, or repetitive.

Can someone else submit a request on my behalf?

Yes, authorized representatives can submit requests. They'll need to provide proof of authorization and go through the same verification process.

What if I don't remember my user ID?

That's okay. You can find your User ID in the app settings under "Account Information" or simply provide your registered email address. We can locate your account using your email.

Can I cancel or modify my request?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your request by replying to the confirmation email we send you. Please include your request ID (provided in the confirmation email) for reference.
