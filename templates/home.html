Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MCQ Solvear

Is MCQ Solvear completely free to use?

Yes, MCQ Solvear is completely free to download and use. All quiz categories, features, and updates are available at no cost.

Can I use the app offline?

You need an internet connection for initial authentication and to download quiz categories. Once downloaded, you can practice quizzes offline.

How often are new questions added?

We regularly update our question database with new quizzes across all categories. Updates are pushed through app updates.

Is my progress saved if I change devices?

Yes, your progress is synchronized with your account. Simply log in on your new device to continue where you left off.
